# Phoenix Product Principles

1. Truth before engagement.
2. Never fabricate history, science, culture, or geography.
3. Clearly label folklore, interpretation, and disputed claims.
4. Use original or properly licensed images, fonts, audio, and visual assets.
5. Chinese is the primary learning content; translation is optional assistance.
6. Story difficulty must adapt to Explorer ability.
7. Every unfamiliar word should be explainable without leaving the app.
8. Learning history is saved automatically.
9. AI must respond to Explorer input and explain improvements respectfully.
10. Free Explorers may access all journeys; paid membership only increases daily exploration capacity.
11. No third-party advertising.
12. Every journey should create language learning, curiosity, and a memory.
